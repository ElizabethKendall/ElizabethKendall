Hello! I'm Elizabeth.

I like to listen, learn, and create sustainable solutions to problems.

I earned a degree in Civil Engineering with an Environmental focus at Seattle University, a degree program I chose to take to learn technical problem-solving skills and social responsibility. Following graduation, pursuing a career in Civil Engineering was sidelined by a car accident.

Since then, I've applied my programming skills to solving business problems. Solving problems with code became a passion.

Thanks to Coding Dojo, mentorship and many hours of self-directed study, I have a new set of full-stack web design skills with which to solve problems.

My core professional goals are being able to contribute value, lifelong learning and growing as a developer.
